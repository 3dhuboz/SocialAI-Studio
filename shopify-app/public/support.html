What to include when you email

So we can resolve quickly, please include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead, including any error message
  • Approximate time the issue occurred (helps us correlate logs)
  • Optional: a screenshot

Common questions

How do I cancel?

From your Shopify admin: Settings → Apps → SocialAI Studio → Uninstall. Your subscription cancels automatically. Per Shopify’s policy, charges already incurred in the current billing period are not refunded, but you won’t be charged again.

Is my customer data shared?

No. We request read_products only — we never see customer names, emails, addresses, orders, or payment info. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens to my data if I uninstall?

Shopify sends us a shop/redact webhook 48 hours after uninstall. At that point we permanently delete all data associated with your shop. The 48-hour window is Shopify’s grace period in case you reinstall.

Why is my charge in test mode?

Development stores (those with partner_test plans) are automatically billed in test mode. You won’t see a real charge unless you’re on a paid Shopify plan.

The install loop keeps failing — what should I do?

Email us with your shop domain and approximate time of the failure. We’ll check the logs and respond same business day. Most install issues resolve with a single uninstall + reinstall, but we’ll diagnose the actual cause.

For Shopify reviewers

If you’re a Shopify App Store reviewer, the install flow uses Token Exchange (Managed Install). Merchants land directly in the embedded app at app.socialaistudio.au, exchange their session token for an offline access token, and proceed to billing approval. Our privacy policy details data handling. Reach out to steve@pennywiseit.com.au for any review questions.
